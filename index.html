About us


GlobalHunt Foundation (registered as Section 8 under The Companies Act, 1956) which works with businesses, government, social organizations, academias, institutions, funders, innovators etc. who believe in developing and implementing sustainable models that are relevant to overcome the disparity of society.

We assist in building models of interventions which bridge the gap between inequality to equality, unsustainable to sustainable, impractical to practical, impossible to possible.
