PRIVACY POLICY



At GlobalHunt Foundation, we respect & protect the privacy of the users & visitors of our website. This Privacy Policy Statement is formulated with a purpose to explain the practices we maintain for protecting the privacy preferences of the individuals visiting our website www.globalhuntfoundation.org.


The Privacy Policy document also states on what processes we follow for collection of information and practices sharing. There are various formats (website, phone, social media or any other mode) through which we collect & receive personal information, therefore we make all efforts to protect your privacy. Hence in this document GlobalHunt Foundation states why & how it collects and the way GlobalHunt Foundation protects users’ information.


Information we collect from you.


Any user or visitor entering the website www.globalhuntfoundation.org is not required to share any information about themselves. However, in certain sections of the website we will need to collect your personal data in order to proceed further on the services we provide.


  • Personal Information

Throughout the website we have created content that are meant for information purposes and does not require a visitor or user to share their personal information. Moreover in certain sections we have created a form which allows a user to voluntary share their information. Therefore information of this kind will only be collected when you contact us directly by an email or phone or when you specifically request that we share information to you.


In any form through our website or social media platforms we do not promote, generate or create links & platforms that requires and allows a user to undertake financial transactions.

  • Anonymised user information

We do not collect any user information which anonymous nor do we use cookies on the website of GlobalHunt Foundation.

  • Why we collect personal information from you

Your personal information is needed so that we can contact you only when you have asked us to do so through our contact us form available on the website.

  • How we use your personal information

The personal information shared by you with us are solely meant to share and supply information about our services as and when requested by you. Furthermore, whenever and where ever consented by you we may use your contact information to update you about our services which might be relevant to you. Moreover in case you want to get in touch with us for any services that might interest you can get in touch with us through our contact us form, or email or phone number mentioned in the website.

The personal information we collect through our website or social media platforms may include, but not limited to, visitor’s name, postal address, date of birth, PIN code, telephone & mobile phone numbers, organization’s name and email addresses.

  • Protection of your personal information

In all the cases, the personal information provided to you by us will be maintained & secured on our systems and may be only accessed by or given to individuals working for GlobalHunt Foundation.

If GlobalHunt Foundation, in the future, enters into a joint venture or is sold or merged to a third party entity, then your personal information may be disclosed & shared to those who own, control and work for the third party entity.

Unless and until required by law, we will not share, distribute, promote or sale any of your information shared by you during our interactions over website, email, phone or through any other platform.

We diligently employ security systems & practices to protect your personal information being vulnerable & accessible to any unauthorized persons, systems and against unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction and damage. We will retain your information for a reasonable period or as long as the law requires.

  • Accessing and changing your personal information

You are entitled to see the information retained by us about you and accordingly you may ask us to make any changes in t so as to ensure that it is accurate. In case you want to change the preferences, you may have previously expressed, about the way we can use your personal information then you can contact us at corporate@globalhuntfoundation.org

  • Changes to our privacy policy

Any changes to our privacy policy in the future will be posted on the website www.globalhuntfoundation.org and wherever appropriate & required will be informed to you through e-mail notification. Otherwise the changes will be immediately effective upon posting on the website.

As and when required & decided, GlobalHunt Foundation will alter its privacy practices in accordance with the latest technology, trends and laws.

GlobalHunt Foundation (GHF) is a Section 8 registered not-for profit organization working towards implementing diverse CSR programmes in various thematic clusters as per The Schedule VII, of the Companies Act, 2013.
