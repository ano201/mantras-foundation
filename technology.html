Mantras Technology Forum



Welcome to our NGO! We are dedicated to harnessing the power of technology to improve the lives of individuals and communities around the world. Our mission is to provide access to technology and technology-related education and resources to individuals who may not have had access otherwise.

We believe that technology has the power to transform lives and create opportunities for individuals and communities. Our programs are designed to provide individuals with the skills and resources they need to thrive in a digital world, from basic digital literacy to advanced technical skills.

Our focus is on providing education and training in a wide range of technology-related skills, from basic computer skills to advanced programming and robotics. We work with individuals, communities, and governments to identify the technology-related skills that are in demand in different regions and to provide the training and support needed to develop those skills.

Education is a key focus of our work. We believe that education is essential for individuals to succeed in a digital world and achieve their full potential. Through our education programs, we provide individuals with access to high-quality education in technology-related fields, including coding, digital marketing, data analysis, and cybersecurity.

In addition to education, we also provide access to technology resources, including computer labs, internet access, and technology equipment. We believe that access to technology is essential for individuals to fully participate in a digital world and take advantage of the opportunities that technology presents.

Our work also includes the development of technology-based solutions to address social and economic challenges faced by communities. We believe that technology has the potential to create innovative solutions to some of the world's most pressing problems, and we are committed to working with communities to develop and implement these solutions.

At our NGO, we are committed to harnessing the power of technology to improve the lives of individuals and communities around the world. We believe that by providing access to technology and technology-related education and resources, we can create opportunities for individuals to thrive in a digital world and contribute to the growth and development of their communities. Join us in our mission to empower individuals and communities through technology.

GlobalHunt Foundation (GHF) is a Section 8 registered not-for profit organization working towards implementing diverse CSR programmes in various thematic clusters as per The Schedule VII, of the Companies Act, 2013.
